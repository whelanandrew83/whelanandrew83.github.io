Tasmanian COVID exposure sites

Changes to exposure site listings in Tasmania
Please note that, as per coronavirus.tas.gov.au, the Tasmanian Department of Health will no longer be publishing exposure sites. As such, this page will no longer be updated.

There are 282 exposure sites in Tasmania, including 9 added on 30 December 2021, based on information downloaded from coronavirus.tas.gov.au at 12:30 PM on 31 December 2021.

Whilst every effort is made to ensure the information on this page is an accurate representation of the information on the Tasmanian Government coronavirus website, please refer to coronavirus.tas.gov.au for official details of public exposure sites in Tasmania.

Elo Race

The Elo Race is an attempt to rate a player’s season using some elo rating concepts. A player is awarded points for each match based on the result (win or loss) and their opponent’s elo rating, but not the player’s own rating.

A value of 1700 is used for the player’s own rating in the elo formula (outlined in the section above).

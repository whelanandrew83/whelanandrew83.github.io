Abbreviations

General

  • Players: Total players used, including medical subs who have not played any game time.
  • Age: Average age of players in each match played.
  • Exp.: Average career games experience for players in each match.
  • Rating: Official AFL Player Ratings (Overview)

Disposals

  • Kick: Kicks
  • HB: Handballs
  • Dis.: Disposals
  • Eff %: Disposal Efficiency
  • Kick Eff %: Kick Efficiency
  • Kick %: Kicks / Disposals
  • D / O Tk: Disposals / Opposition Tackle
  • In 50s: Inside 50s
  • Reb 50s: Rebound 50s
  • R50 Rate: Rebound 50s per Opposition Inside 50
  • Mtrs Gnd: Metres gained
  • Mtrs / D: Metres gained per Disposal
  • Clng: Clangers
  • D/Cg: Disposals per clanger
  • TO: Turnovers
  • D/TO: Disposals per turnover

Possessions

  • CP: Contested possessions
  • UP: Uncontested possessions
  • Poss: Total possessions
  • CP%: Contested possessions / total possessions
  • Int Poss: Intercept possessions
  • GB Gets: Ground ball gets
  • Hard Gets: Hard ball gets. A disputed ball at ground level under direct physical pressure or out of a ruck contest, resulting in an opportunity to effect a legal disposal.
  • Loose Gets: Loose ball gets. A disputed ball at ground level not under direct physical pressure that results in an opportunity to record a legal disposal.
  • Gath HO: Gathers from hitout. A possession gained from a teammate’s hitout-to-advantage
  • Crumb: Crumbing possessions. Ground ball gets that are won by a player at ground level after a marking contest.
  • HB Rec: Handball receives

Clearances

  • Ctr Clr: Centre clearances
  • Stp Clr: Stoppage clearances
  • Tot: Total clearances

Marks

  • Tot: Total marks
  • CM: Contested marks
  • In 50: Marks inside forward 50
  • M50 Rate: Marks inside forward 50 per inside 50
  • Int Mks: Intercept marks
  • On lead: Marks on lead

Scoring

  • Goal Sc.: Total goal scorers.
  • Avg GS: Average goal scorers per match.
  • Gls: Goals
  • Beh: Behinds (includes rushed behinds)
  • RB: Rushed behinds
  • Shots: Shots at goal
  • Goal Ass.: Goal assists
  • Acc: Accuracy (Goals / shots at goal)
  • G / In 50: Goals / Inside 50
  • SS / In 50: Scoring shots / Inside 50 (includes rushed behinds)
  • S / In 50: Shots at goal / Inside 50
  • SI: Score involvements
  • Launch: Score launches
  • Off 1v1: Offensive one-on-one contests
  • Win %: Percentage of offensive one-on-one contests won

Expected Scores (xScores)

  • xSc: Average expected score per game (excludes rushed behinds).
  • +/-: Average score above or below expected per game (excludes rushed behinds).
  • xSc / Shot: Average expected score per shot at goal, reflecting the average difficulty of shots taken.
  • Rating: Average score above or below expected score per shot at goal.
  • xSc / Sh (Set): Average expected score per shot at goal for set shots, reflecting the average difficulty of shots taken.
  • Rating (Set): Average score above or below expected score per shot at goal for set shots.
  • xSc / Sh (Gen): Average expected score per shot at goal for shots in general play, reflecting the average difficulty of shots taken.
  • Rating (Gen): Average score above or below expected score per shot at goal for shots in general play.

Score Sources

  • Kick In: Points scored from kick-ins.
  • Stop: Points scored from stoppages.
  • Turnover: Points scored from turnovers.

Score Origin

  • Def Half: Points scored from defensive half.
  • Fwd Half: Points scored from forward half.
  • Ctr Bnc: Points scored from centre bounce.

Defence

  • Def 1v1: Defensive one-on-one contests
  • Loss %: Percentage of defensive one-on-one contests lost
  • Tack: Total tackles
  • Tack In 50: Tackles inside forward 50
  • Press. Acts: Pressure acts

Ruck contests

  • Ruck Cont: Total ruck contests
  • Hit Outs: Total hit outs
  • Win %: Hit outs / ruck contests
  • To Adv: Hit outs to advantage
  • Adv %: Hit outs to advantage / total hit outs
  • Ruck HBG: Ruck hard ball gets. Taking possession of the ball directly out of the ruck.

Other

  • KI Play On %: Play on percentage from kick-ins
  • Bounce: Bounces
  • 1%s: One percenters

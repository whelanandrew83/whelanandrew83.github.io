Abbreviations

Summary

  • Player Rating: Official AFL Player Ratings (Overview)

Disposals

  • K: Kicks
  • H: Handballs
  • D: Disposals
  • Eff %: Disposal Efficiency
  • Kick Eff %: Kick Efficiency
  • Kick %: Kicks / Disposals
  • D / O Tk: Disposals / Opposition Tackle
  • In 50s: Inside 50s
  • Reb 50s: Rebound 50s
  • R50 Rate: Rebound 50s per Opposition Inside 50
  • Mtrs Gnd: Metres gained
  • Mtrs / D: Metres gained per Disposal
  • Clng: Clangers
  • D/Cg: Disposals per clanger
  • TO: Turnovers
  • D/TO: Disposals per turnover

Possessions

  • CP: Contested possessions
  • UP: Uncontested possessions
  • Poss: Total possessions
  • CP%: Contested possessions / total possessions
  • Int: Intercept possessions
  • GB Gets: Ground ball gets

Clearances

  • Ctr Clr: Centre clearances
  • Stp Clr: Stoppage clearances
  • Tot: Total clearances

Marks

  • Tot: Total marks
  • CM: Contested marks
  • In 50: Marks inside forward 50
  • M50 Rate: Marks inside forward 50 per inside 50
  • Int: Intercept marks
  • On lead: Marks on lead

Ruck contests

  • Tot: Total ruck contests
  • HO: Total hit outs
  • Win %: Hit outs / ruck contests
  • To Adv: Hit outs to advantage
  • Adv %: Hit outs to advantage / total hit outs

Tackles

  • Tot: Total tackles
  • In 50: Tackles inside forward 50

Contests

  • Def 1v1: Defensive one-on-one contests
  • Loss %: Percentage of defensive one-on-one contests lost
  • Off 1v1: Offensive one-on-one contests
  • Win %: Percentage of offensive one-on-one contests won

Scoring

  • Gls: Goals
  • Beh: Behinds
  • RB: Rushed behinds
  • Shots: Shots at goal
  • Goal Ass.: Goal assists
  • Acc: Accuracy (Goals / shots at goal)
  • G / In 50: Goals / Inside 50
  • SS / In 50: Scoring Shots / Inside 50 (includes rushed behinds)
  • S / In 50: Shots at goal / Inside 50
  • SI: Score involvements
  • Launch: Score launches

Other

  • Play On %: Play on percentage from kick-ins
  • Bounce: Bounces
  • 1%s: One percenters
  • Press. Acts: Pressure acts
